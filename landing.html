Features

Everything you need to connect

From real-time messaging to Spotify music sync, ZenChat is packed with features that make every conversation richer.

Real-time Messaging
Instant message delivery powered by Socket.io. See when friends are typing and messages appear the moment they're sent.
Spotify Music Sharing
Share what you're listening to directly in the chat. Spotify track previews appear beautifully inline in every conversation.
Group Conversations
Create group chats with custom names and avatars. Add members, collaborate, and keep everyone in the loop.
Media Sharing
Send images and videos stored securely on Cloudinary. Files render beautifully in chat with lightbox previews.
Push Notifications
Never miss a message. Web push notifications keep you updated even when you're not on the page, powered by Firebase FCM.
Profile Customization
Upload custom avatars, set display names, and personalize your profile. Your identity, your way.

Security First

Your account,
fully protected.

ZenChat takes your security seriously. Enable email-based two-factor authentication for an extra layer of protection on every login.

  • Email OTP sent on every login when 2FA is enabled
  • OTPs expire automatically after 5 minutes
  • Resend available once per minute to prevent abuse
  • JWT-based sessions with configurable expiry
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt at rest
